Medical Emergency Procedures

What to Do in a Medical Emergency

  1. Stay Calm: Assess the situation quickly but calmly
  2. Call for Help: Dial 998 for ambulance immediately
  3. Provide Information: Give clear location and nature of emergency
  4. First Aid: If trained, provide basic first aid while waiting
  5. Stay with Person: Don't leave the injured person alone

Common Workshop Injuries

Cuts & Lacerations

Apply pressure with clean cloth, elevate wound, seek medical attention for deep cuts

Burns

Cool with water for 10 minutes, cover with clean cloth, seek medical help for severe burns

Electric Shock

Turn off power source, call emergency services, do not touch person if still in contact with electricity

Eye Injuries

Do not rub eyes, flush with clean water, seek immediate medical attention
