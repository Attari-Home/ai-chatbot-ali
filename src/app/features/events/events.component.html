About FIRST Robotics Competition

What is FRC?

The FIRST Robotics Competition is an international high school robotics competition. Teams of students are challenged to design, build, and program robots to compete in a series of challenges.

Season Structure

  • Kickoff: Teams receive the game challenge and start building
  • Build Season: 6 weeks to design and construct robots
  • Competition Season: Regional and district events
  • Championship: World Championship event
