Our Story

The ALI Robotics Team was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between theoretical STEM education and practical application. What started as a small group of enthusiastic students has grown into a thriving community of innovators, engineers, and problem-solvers.

Our journey began when a group of dedicated educators and technology enthusiasts recognized the need for hands-on robotics education in our region. Today, we compete in international robotics competitions, collaborate on cutting-edge projects, and inspire countless students to pursue careers in STEM fields.
